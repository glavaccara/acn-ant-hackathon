Why your risk team will actually approve it

Compliance signs off once.

Most "AI agents" rely on telling the model not to do things. Ours cannot do them. Dangerous actions don't exist as options. Risky ones are gated by code your team controls — not by a prompt the model can talk its way around.

06
Where it costs us — honestly

Three trade-offs you should know before you sign.

Per-call cost is real

A premium model isn't free. We mitigate by using a smaller, cheaper model for ~80% of the work — only the orchestrator pays premium pricing.

Latency is a UX constraint

Heavy answers take 3–6 seconds. Fine for "advise me", too slow for transaction-time UI. We design around it: streaming responses, async cleanup tasks.

One vendor, one model family

If Anthropic has a bad day, we have a bad day. Bedrock provides AWS-side resilience; the math behind affordability still runs even if the model is offline.

We're showing you these because your risk team will ask anyway. Better the answers come from us first.
